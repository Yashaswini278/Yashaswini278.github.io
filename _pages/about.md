Hi, I'm Yashaswini, a final-year student at the [Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur](https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/), where I’m pursuing an Integrated Masters in Electrical Engineering (BTech) and Machine Learning and Signal Processing (MTech).

My research focuses on the intersection of Deep Learning, Computational Neuroscience, and Signal Processing. I am particularly passionate about exploring how neuroscience principles can inspire advancements in deep learning.

Currently, I'm interning at TU Eindhoven in the [NeurAI lab](https://github.com/NeurAI-Lab) under the guidance of Dr. Bahram Zonooz and Dr. Elahe Arani, where I’m developing neuroscience-inspired models for continual learning. As part of my Masters thesis, I’m also investigating Functional Connectome-based Brain Fingerprinting under the guidance of Dr. Sanjay Ghosh at IIT Kharagpur. My Bachelors thesis, titled "Emergence of Auditory Receptive Fields based on Surprise at Multiple Timescales," was completed under the mentorship of Dr. Sharba Bandyopadhyay.
